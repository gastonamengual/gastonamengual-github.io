About


Hello there! Welcome to my website. My name is Gastón Amengual, I am a Senior System Engineering Student at the National Technological University in Argentina.
Since 2020, I focus on Data Science, more specifically, on Optimization, Machine Learning, Modeling, and Statistics, and my current goal is to learn the most common algorithms, models, and techniques in those areas. I believe that Data Science is not about importing functions and measure the precision and recall of the results, but rather about understanding how the algorithms and models work, being able to apply them from scratch.
On this website, I show my portfolio, where I describe both complete and on-going projects, along with my articles, which are Jupyter Notebooks on some Data Science topics. Let's get started!
